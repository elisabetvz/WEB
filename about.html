I'm a Graphic Designer
and Art Director
based in LA.

Elisabet is a multidisciplinary Art Director based in Los Angeles. Her work springs from a spirit of relentless passion, curiosity for the unseen, emerging at the intersection of fashion, popular culture and social change. She is driven by her passion to tell the stories that are yet to be told. Her work springs from a spirit of relentless passion, curiosity for the unseen, emerging at the intersection of fashion, popular culture and social change. The intentionality in her work is driven by her passion to tell the stories that would remain hidden.

Her work ranges from Retail Advertisign, Agency Art Direction, to non-profit campaigns and social media development. Her most recent experience is working behind the scenes of Juice House Collective, producing and creative directing projects for artists, models, and a variety of talent.


She is the host of 'Amateur', a podcast to learn how to turn life lessons into the material for growth. We are all in a journey of becoming and the best way to progress is to fall in love with the process. It’s never too late to be an amateur of life..
