Elisabet Vázquez is a Spanish Designer and Art Director working independently in Los Angeles.

She specializes in branding, editorial design and art direction. With a keen eye for detail, her work is known for its thoughtfulness and warmth.

With agency and client experiences across a range of industries including fashion, beauty, hospitality, luxury and publishing, she has had the opportunity to work with brands and studios such as Juice House, Mosaic and Mina Baie.

Contact

Stefanie is available for design, art direction and creative direction projects.

As a sustainability focused and ethically minded person, she is especially interested to work on projects with brands in the fashion, beauty, hospitality, food & beverage, interior design & home goods, luxury and publishing industries that are kind to our planet and respect all who reside on it.

When inquiring about potential projects and collaborations, please include information about your company and project, desired timeline and budget.

For all business inquiries, collaborations, press features or speaking engagements, please email: hello@stefaniebrueckler.com
