Elisabet is a multidisciplinary Art Director and Designer based in Los Angeles.
Her work comes from a spirit of curiosity and sits at the intersection of fashion, digital and youth culture.
She aims to offer a subversive means of cultural reflection through her work; grappling
with mainstream consciousness and our collective understanding of youth and identity.

New York has been her home for the past 11 years, most recently running the Global
advertising design department for adidas Originals at Johannes Leonardo.
